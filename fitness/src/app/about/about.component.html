About Fitness Tracker

FitTrack is a personal fitness tracking application designed to help you set, track, and achieve your workout goals. Whether you're aiming to burn calories, increase workout duration, or stay consistent with your exercise routine — this app makes it easy.

You can set fitness goals by selecting exercise types, target calories, and durations. Your progress is tracked automatically and stored securely using CouchDB.

Key Features

🎯
Set personalized fitness goals with specific targets
📊
Track your progress with detailed analytics
